Our Achievements




Annual Computer Science Internship Recruitment Fair



The Annual Computer Science Internship Recruitment Fair is one of the USM Computer Science Society's key achievements, aiming to bridge the gap between students and industry professionals. This event is held annually, offering a platform for students to connect with leading tech companies, learn about potential career opportunities, and gain insights into industry trends.
Through this fair, the society collaborates with top companies from various sectors, including software development, AI, cybersecurity, and data science. Students can participate in on-the-spot interviews, attend company presentations, and explore internship openings that provide real-world experience in their respective fields of study.
The fair serves as a crucial step for students to launch their careers, providing them with the chance to secure internships, build professional networks, and gain insights into the expectations and skills required in the tech industry. It is a testament to the society's commitment to empowering students and preparing them for successful careers in technology.


STEM Outreach Program



The STEM Outreach Program spearheaded by the USM Computer Science Society is a community-driven initiative that aims to inspire and educate young minds about the importance of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) from an early age. Targeting primary school students in Penang, the program brings STEM concepts to life through hands-on activities, workshops, and interactive learning experiences.

By partnering with local schools, the society organizes coding workshops, robotics competitions, and introductory lessons in data science and artificial intelligence. These workshops are designed to engage students in creative problem-solving, critical thinking, and teamwork, empowering them to pursue STEM-related subjects as they progress in their education. The outreach program also emphasizes the role of technology in solving real-world problems, helping to cultivate a passion for innovation and science in young learners. By nurturing a new generation of STEM enthusiasts, the society aims to foster diversity and inclusion in the technology sector, especially among underrepresented groups.


Campus Mobility Projects



The Campus Mobility Projects undertaken by the USM Computer Science Society aim to enhance the efficiency and sustainability of transportation systems within the university campus. These projects are designed to address the challenges associated with campus mobility, including traffic congestion, environmental concerns, and student accessibility.

One of the key initiatives under this project is the development of smart transportation solutions such as campus shuttle tracking systems, mobile apps for real-time transportation updates, and automated bike-sharing services. These systems help students and faculty navigate the campus more efficiently, reducing time spent in transit and minimizing the carbon footprint of campus commuting.

The society has also worked on promoting sustainable mobility through the implementation of electric vehicle (EV) charging stations and integrating green transportation options like electric bikes and scooters into the campus.
