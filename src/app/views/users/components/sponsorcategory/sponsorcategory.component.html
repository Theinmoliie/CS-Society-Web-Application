Sponsorship Categories

The following information outlines the sponsorship categories and their respective perks.

Platinum Sponsor (Highest Tier)

  • Logo Placement: Premium placement on all event banners, posters, and digital materials.
  • Acknowledgment: Special mention during opening and closing ceremonies.
  • Social Media Feature: Dedicated post on all official social media pages.
  • Booth Space: Priority access to set up a booth during the event.
  • Certificate of Appreciation: Personalized platinum-tier certificate.
  • Free Passes: Complimentary event tickets (up to 5).

Silver Sponsor

  • Logo Placement: Medium-sized logo placement on banners and digital materials.
  • Acknowledgment: Mention in sponsor appreciation slides during the event.
  • Social Media Feature: Logo included in a group sponsor appreciation post.
  • Certificate of Appreciation: Personalized silver-tier certificate.
  • Free Passes: Complimentary event tickets (up to 2).

Bronze Sponsor

  • Logo Placement: Small logo placement on banners and digital materials.
  • Acknowledgment: Mention in sponsor appreciation slides during the event.
  • Social Media Feature: Name included in a group sponsor appreciation post.
  • Certificate of Appreciation: Personalized bronze-tier certificate.
  • Free Pass: Complimentary event ticket (1).
