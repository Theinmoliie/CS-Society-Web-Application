Empower CS Sponsorship Program

Terms and Conditions

  1. Eligibility: The sponsorship program is open to individuals, organizations, and businesses that wish to support initiatives, events, and activities aimed at advancing the Computer Science community at USM.
  2. Use of Logo and Branding: Sponsors permit the Empower CS program to use their company logo, name, and branding in relation to the program, events, and promotional materials.
  3. Payment: All payments must be made in full upon agreement to confirm sponsorship. The payment must be completed as per the details provided in the program.
  4. Refund Policy: Sponsorship fees are non-refundable once payment has been made and confirmed.
  5. Acknowledgment: Sponsors will receive acknowledgment through various channels, including event materials and social media, as outlined in the sponsorship package.
  6. Cancellation: Empower CS reserves the right to cancel or modify the program in the event of unforeseen circumstances.
