FAQ Section

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the Empower CS Sponsorship Program?

The Empower CS Sponsorship Program supports initiatives, events, and activities that promote excellence and inclusivity within the Computer Science community at USM.

2. Who can participate as a sponsor?

Individuals, organizations, and businesses interested in supporting the development of computer science students and empowering future tech leaders are welcome to participate.

3. What are the sponsorship categories?

We offer various sponsorship categories, each with unique perks, ranging from logo placement on event materials to VIP access at our events. Please refer to the sponsorship package for detailed options.

4. How can I make the payment?

Payment details will be provided upon selecting your sponsorship category. Please follow the instructions to make the payment through the specified channels.

5. Can I modify my sponsorship after committing?

Once confirmed, sponsorship details, including payment, are non-refundable. However, you may contact us for any special requests, and we will try to accommodate them.

6. What if I have further questions after submitting my sponsorship?

If you have any additional questions, our team will be happy to assist. You can reach us via the contact details provided in your confirmation email.

7. How can I stay updated on the event and program details?

You will receive regular updates via email. We encourage sponsors to check their emails frequently for any important information or next steps related to the program.
